Questions

Pricing FAQs

Are these prices final or can they change? +
The listed prices are starting prices for standard requirements. After a free consultation call, we'll send you a detailed quote based on your specific project scope. We never add costs after the quote is agreed — what we quote is what you pay.
Do you offer EMI or payment in installments? +
Yes! For projects above ₹15,000, we offer flexible payment structures: typically 40% advance, 40% at design approval, and 20% on final delivery. For enterprise projects we can arrange monthly billing through milestones. Contact us to discuss.
Is domain & hosting included in the price? +
Domain registration and hosting are typically purchased in the client's name for full ownership. We guide you through the best options (usually ₹500–₹3,000/year for domain and ₹3,000–₹8,000/year for hosting depending on traffic). We handle the full setup at no extra charge.
What if I need features not listed in a package? +
Every business is unique. If you need features outside a standard package — like custom booking systems, inventory modules, payment portals, or advanced integrations — we scope it separately and provide a transparent add-on quote. Nothing gets built without your approval first.
How long does a website project take? +
Timelines depend on complexity: Starter sites (5 pages) take 7–10 business days; Growth sites take 14–18 days; Full-stack applications take 25–40 days. These timelines assume timely content and feedback from your side. We share a detailed project timeline on kickoff.
Can I cancel an ongoing monthly service? +
Monthly services (SEO, ads, maintenance, automation) require 30 days' notice before cancellation. There are no lock-in contracts. We earn your business every month through results — if we're not delivering, you're free to leave.
