Website Development

Building, launching, and maintaining websites

Will my website be mobile-friendly? +
100%. Every website we build is mobile-first — meaning we design and develop for mobile screens first, then scale up to desktop. With over 70% of Indian internet traffic coming from mobile devices, this is non-negotiable for us. We test across iOS, Android, and all major screen sizes before delivery.
Do you use templates or build custom websites? +
We build fully custom websites from scratch. No Themeforest themes, no recycled layouts. Every design is created specifically for your brand, industry, and target audience. Custom builds perform better in SEO, load faster, and stand out more than template sites which often look identical to competitors.
Do you provide content (text & images) for the website? +
The best websites use the client's real content — your services, photos, and messaging. We provide a content brief guiding you on exactly what to share. However, if you need copywriting or professional imagery, we offer:
  • SEO copywriting for all pages (+add-on)
  • Professional stock photography sourcing
  • Brand logo and basic graphic design
Ask us to include these in your quote.
Will I be able to update my website myself after launch? +
Yes, for WordPress-based projects you get full admin access and can update content, images, and blog posts independently. For custom-built applications, we provide a simple admin CMS panel. We also provide a video walkthrough showing how to manage your website after handover. And if you ever need changes, our maintenance plans have you covered.
Who owns the website once it's built? +
You own 100% of your website — domain, code, hosting, and all content. Domain and hosting are registered in your name. We deliver all source code upon final payment. You're never locked into using our services to keep the site running.
How fast will my website load? +
Speed is a priority. All our websites are optimized for Core Web Vitals — the Google ranking signals related to speed and user experience. Typical results: under 2-second load time, 90+ PageSpeed score. We achieve this through lazy loading, image compression, minification, caching, and CDN setup.

Marketing & SEO

Rankings, ads, and growing online visibility

How long does SEO take to show results? +
SEO is a long-term investment. You can typically expect to see:
  • Months 1–2: Technical fixes, indexation improvements, early ranking signals
  • Months 3–4: Noticeable improvements for long-tail and local keywords
  • Months 4–6: Page 1 rankings for primary keywords begin
  • Month 6+: Sustained organic traffic growth and compounding returns
Beware any agency promising "page 1 in 2 weeks" — that's either paid ads being misrepresented, or black-hat tactics that will eventually get your site penalized.
What is the minimum budget I should allocate for Google or Meta Ads? +
For meaningful results, we recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹10,000– ₹15,000 per month per platform. Below this, the data collected is too limited to optimize effectively. Our management fee is separate from your ad budget. For local businesses with tight budgets, we'll recommend the platform where your audience is most active to maximize impact.
Do you guarantee Google page 1 rankings? +
No reputable SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings — Google's algorithm changes constantly and there are hundreds of factors involved. What we guarantee is: thorough strategy, complete transparency, white-hat methods that won't put your site at risk, and proven results. Our case studies show consistent page 1 rankings achieved within 4–6 months for targeted keywords.
What kind of reports will I receive for my campaigns? +
We believe in full transparency. You receive:
  • Monthly SEO reports: keyword rankings, traffic data, backlinks acquired
  • Weekly ad reports: impressions, clicks, conversions, cost-per-lead
  • Monthly strategy reviews: what worked, what we're optimizing, next steps
All reports are in plain English — no jargon, no vanity metrics. Just numbers that connect to your business goals.

Pricing & Payment

Costs, payment methods, and billing

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major payment methods including UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS), and for larger projects, we can issue formal invoices. We provide GST-compliant invoices for all payments. International clients can pay via Wise or bank wire transfer.
Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied? +
We have a milestone-based payment structure specifically to protect both parties. The advance payment covers initial design work. We work through revision rounds to ensure you're happy before proceeding to each next stage. If we cannot deliver what was agreed in the contract, we will refund the relevant payment portion. Full terms are in our Terms of Service.
Are there any recurring costs after the website is built? +
The website development fee is a one-time cost. However, to keep your website live and secure, you'll need:
  • Domain renewal: ~₹800–₹1,500/year (paid to registrar)
  • Web hosting: ~₹3,000–₹8,000/year (paid to hosting provider)
  • SSL certificate: usually included with hosting
  • Maintenance plan (optional): starts at ₹1,999/mo
We'll give you a complete cost estimate for year 1 and beyond during our consultation.
Do you provide GST invoices? +
Yes, we provide proper GST-compliant invoices for all our services. This ensures you can claim input tax credit (ITC) where applicable for your business. Our GSTIN is available on all invoices.

Process & Support

How we work and how we support you

What does the onboarding process look like? +
Our process is straightforward:
  • Step 1: Free 30-min strategy call to understand your goals
  • Step 2: We share a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing within 24 hours
  • Step 3: Agreement signed + advance payment to kick off
  • Step 4: Content brief sent — you provide your materials
  • Step 5: Design → Review → Development → Testing → Launch
How do you communicate during the project? +
We communicate primarily via WhatsApp and email. You'll have a dedicated point of contact — not a rotating team of strangers. We do regular progress updates, and you can reach us Monday–Saturday, 9 AM–8 PM IST. For urgent issues, we respond within 2 hours.
What happens if the project takes longer than estimated? +
We plan meticulously and 95% of our projects are delivered on schedule. If a delay occurs on our side, we communicate proactively and never charge extra for time overruns caused by us. If delays occur due to late content or feedback from your side, we'll reschedule the timeline accordingly and notify you in advance.
Do you provide post-launch support? +
Yes. Every project comes with a free post-launch support period: 7 days for Starter sites, 30 days for Growth sites, and 60 days for Full-Stack projects. During this period, any bugs or issues found are fixed at no charge. After the free support period, you can continue with one of our affordable maintenance plans.
Can I see a design mockup before the full project starts? +
Absolutely. Our process includes a design approval stage where we present your homepage design (and key page layouts for larger projects) before writing a single line of code. You'll provide feedback, we revise, and only proceed to development once you're 100% happy with the visual direction. This protects your investment and ensures the final product matches your vision.
