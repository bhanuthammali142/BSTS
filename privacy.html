1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you fill in our contact form, project enquiry form, or communicate with us, we collect:

  • Full name and business name
  • Phone number and email address
  • Project details, budget range, and service requirements you share
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide in messages

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, our hosting and analytics tools may automatically collect:

  • IP address and general geographic location (city/country level)
  • Browser type and version, operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation paths
  • Referring website or search query that brought you to us
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)

Information from Third-Party Integrations

If you contact us via WhatsApp Business, LinkedIn, or Instagram, information shared on those platforms is subject to their respective privacy policies. We store only the information necessary to respond to your enquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To respond to your enquiries, prepare proposals, and deliver agreed services
  • Communication: To send project updates, invoices, and service-related information
  • Marketing: With your consent, to send relevant updates about our services (you can opt out any time)
  • Improvement: To analyze website usage and improve our user experience
  • Legal: To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted tools we use to run our business, such as Google Workspace (email), Google Sheets (lead management), and WhatsApp Business — each governed by their own privacy policies
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority in India
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with appropriate notice
  • Your Consent: In any other circumstance, only with your explicit consent

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality (e.g., remembering your form progress)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics 4 to understand website usage (anonymized data)
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality. We do not use cookies for advertising tracking or cross-site behavioral profiling.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Our measures include:

  • HTTPS / SSL encryption on all website communications
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data within our team
  • Secure storage of lead data in password-protected Google Sheets
  • Regular review of third-party tools for security compliance

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately at bhanuthammai26012@gmail.com.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection principles and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Portability: Request your data in a commonly used format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at bhanuthammai26012@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website and operations use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics 4 — Website usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Workspace — Email and document management
  • WhatsApp Business — Client communication (Meta Privacy Policy)
  • Google Apps Script — Lead form processing to Google Sheets

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. For significant changes, we will notify existing clients via email. Your continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
